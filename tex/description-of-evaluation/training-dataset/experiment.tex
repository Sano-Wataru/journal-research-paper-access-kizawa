%
Each participant brought their own laptop and placed it on the desk and was asked to follow a series of steps, from viewing a learning video to answering a questionnaire.
%
The scenario and conditions of the experiment, under which participants viewed a video and took a quiz, were explained.
%
A learning video, a notepad, a quiz about the video content, and a questionnaire were provided to each participant.
%
A participant began viewing the learning video and taking notes upon receiving a signal to start.
The participant stopped viewing and taking notes when the video finished.
%
The participant then took the quiz and completed the questionnaire.
%
Google Forms was used for the quiz and questionnaire.
%
The participants were allowed to refer to their notes during the quiz.
%
After the quiz, they completed the questionnaire.
%
The steps were repeated for a set of learning videos.
The above steps were carried out by all participants.

%
The participants in the experiment were 37 students from Shibaura Institute of Technology.
  %
  Their ages ranged from 19 to 24.

  % The experiment was conducted from December 14th to 20th, 2022.

Three learning videos about structured query language (SQL) were examined~\cite{SQL}.

  The quizzes were administered to ascertain the content of each video.
